Alessandro Lonardi

I am Alessandro Lonardi, a masters student in Mathematical Engingeering at UniPd (Padova, IT), where I also obtained my Bachelor's degree in Physics.
Currently I am working as a research intern on my master's thesis at Max Planck Institute for Intelligent Systems (Tübingen, DE), under the supervision of Dott. Caterina De Bacco.
The main focus of my research is developing optimal transport models combining approaches stemming from network routing and ramified optimal transportation.
