Alessandro Lonardi

I am Alessandro Lonardi, a Research Intern at Max Planck Institute for Intelligent Systems (Tübingen, Germany), working under the supervision of Dr. Caterina De Bacco.
The main focus of my current research is studying routing problems combining approaches stemming from optimal transport and belief propagation.
Previously I was a Master's Student in Mathematical Engineering at UniPd (Padua, Italy), where I also obtained my Bachelor's degree in Physics.
